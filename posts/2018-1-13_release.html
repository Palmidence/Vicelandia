Avicus Rank Transfer by dev_revs

  1. Home
  2. Avicus Rank Transfer

Posted on January 13th, 2018

Today we have some exciting news to announce for the Zildith Network. To support our past Avicus players, we have decided to open up rank trials to players who previously had donor ranks on the Avicus Network. We will have five donation ranks on the Zildith Network: Alpha, Delta, Gamma, Omega, and Zeta. In order to find out what trial ranks you are eligible for, find your Avicus rank in the list below. Additionally, if you get a trial rank and then decide to purchase a rank, you will get 20% off your rank purchase (except for Zeta rank).

Iron

If you currently have the “Iron” rank on Avicus, you will be receiving a three week Trial of the “Alpha” rank. The Alpha rank includes the following perks:

  • Join full matches
  • Access to three special badges
  • 5 total Cases*
  • Special in-game and forum tag
  • Access to Discord donator chat
Gold

If you currently have the “Gold” rank on Avicus, you will get the choice of receiving either a three week trial of the “Alpha” rank, or a two week trial of the “Delta” rank. The Delta rank includes the following perks:

  • Join full matches
  • Access to five special badges
  • 10 total Cases*
  • 1.25x Zoins multiplier
  • Special in-game and forum tag
  • Access to Discord donator chat
  • Pick your own team
  • Control music bot in Discord
Emerald

If you currently have the “Emerald” rank on Avicus, you will get the choice of receiving either a three week trial of the “Delta” rank, or a two week trial of the “Gamma” rank. The Gamma rank includes the following perks:

  • Join full matches
  • Access to five special badges
  • 15 total Cases*
  • 1.50x zoins multiplier
  • Special in-game and forum tag
  • Access to Discord donator chat
  • Pick your own team
  • Control music bot in Discord
  • Ability to participate in Beta testing
  • /setnext one map every 5 days
Diamond/Bedrock

If you currently have either the “Diamond” or “Bedrock” rank on Avicus, you will get the choice of receiving either a three week trial of the “Gamma” rank, or a two week trial of the “Omega” rank. The Omega rank includes the following perks:

  • Join full matches
  • Access to five special badges
  • 20 total Cases*
  • 1.75x zoins multiplier
  • Special in-game and forum tag
  • Access to Discord donator chat
  • Pick your own team
  • Control music bot in Discord
  • Ability to participate in Beta testing
  • /setnext one map every 3 days
  • Ability to transfer Zoins
  • /nick command
Zeta

The “Zeta” rank is not available to trial. Here is what the Zeta package includes:

  • Join full matches
  • Access to five special badges
  • 20 total Cases*
  • 2.00x zoins multiplier
  • Special in-game and forum tag
  • Access to Discord donator chat
  • Pick your own team
  • Control music bot in Discord
  • Ability to participate in Beta testing
  • /setnext one map every 3 days
  • Ability to transfer Zoins
  • /nick command
  • Fly in lobby
  • Access to Sumo duels. (Please note: this feature may not be complete when the server is released)

*Please note that if you purchase a rank upgrade, cases from your previous rank are counted towards the case total for your new rank (e.g. if you upgrade from Alpha to Delta, you will only get 5 more cases since you already got 5 from Alpha rank and the case total for Delta rank is 10). Cases contain items such as trial ranks, experience, zoins, and setnext tokens.

Donation Package prices

  • Alpha: $9.99
  • Delta: $14.99
  • Gamma: $24.99
  • Omega: $39.99
  • Zeta: $54.99

We will also allow rank upgrades, with the following prices: (when you buy ranks at full price)

  • Alpha-->Delta: $5.00
  • Delta-->Gamma: $10.00
  • Gamma-->Omega $15.00
  • Omega-->Zeta $15.00
